    Account Listing

    Where the report is:

    Accounting->City Ledger

    What the report shows:

    Every city ledger account and their:

    • Account #
    • Location
    • Who to contact
    • The contact’s phone #
    • The contact’s email address

    Advance Sellout

    Where the report is:

    Front Office->Supervisor->Reports

    What the report shows:

    Arrivals and departures for a date range.

    • Arrivals/Departures on each day in the range
    • Number of rooms that will be occupied
    • What the occupancy percentage will be
    • Number of walk-ins

    Aging Report

    Where the report is:

    Accounting->City Ledger

    What the report shows:

    • City ledger accounts and their current balances
    • Total that has been unpaid for 30, 60, and 90 day
      • You can base aging by the invoice date or the folio posting date

    Airport Schedule

    Where the report is:

    Front Office->Reports

    What the report shows:

    All guest's pickup/drop off times for the airport.

    Filter by:

    • Date-range
    • Drop off only
    • Pickup only

    Arrivals

    Where the report is:

    Front Office->Reports

    What the report shows:

    Reservation details for incoming guests:

    • Confirmation #
    • Arrival/departure date
    • # of adults/children
    • Room rate for each res
    • Advance deposits the guest made
    • Packages booked

    Census

    Where the report is:

    Management->Reports

    What the report shows:

    Capacity, statistics, and revenue for a quarter period.

    Capacity

    • # of rooms
    • # of beds and what type of bed

    Statistics

    These show stats for each month, as well as a total for the quarter.

    • # of rooms rented
    • # of adult guests
    • Total # of guests
    • # of guest arrivals
    • Occupancy %
    • Average length of stay (days)
    • Average # of guests

    Revenue

    • Room revenue
    • ADR
    • ADR w/ comps
    • RevPar

    City Ledger Forecast

    Where the report is:

    Sales & Marketing->Reports->Forecast, click City Ledger button on the left

    What the report shows:

    Actual projection of city ledger stays (not a projection/estimation).

    • Account #
    • City Ledger Name
    • Days on which reservations are made using the city ledger
    • Revenue
    • ADR

    City Ledger History

    Where the report is:

    Sales & Marketing->Reports->History click City Ledger button on the left

    What the report shows:

    City ledger account history.

    Month to date, year to date, last year MTD, and last year YTD:

    • Revenue
    • Total bookings
    • ADR
    • Percent variance between this year and last year

    Company Forecast

    Where the report is:

    Sales & Marketing->Reports->Forecast

    What the report shows:

    Actual projection of company stays (not a projection/estimation).

    • Account #
    • Company name
    • Days on which reservations are made with the company
    • Revenue
    • ADR
